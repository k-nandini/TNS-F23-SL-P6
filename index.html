About Quiet Union

Quiet Union is a tool designed to assist the NYC community in discovering suitable places to relax in Union Square Park. It caters to various needs, such as those with sensory issues, individuals seeking ideal date spots, or those interested in learning about the park's diverse communities. Additionally, it provides information about vendors based on the time of day, helping visitors determine if the park is a suitable place for their relaxation.
